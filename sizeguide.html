How to Measure

For the perfect blouse fit, take these measurements while wearing a well-fitted garment. Keep the measuring tape snug but not tight.

1

Bust/Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape parallel to the floor. Ensure the tape isn't too tight - you should be able to breathe comfortably.

2

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, which is the narrowest part of your torso (usually 1-2 inches above your navel). Stand naturally without sucking in.

3

Shoulder Width

Measure from the edge of one shoulder bone to the other, across your upper back. This ensures the blouse sits correctly on your frame.

4

Blouse Length

Measure from the shoulder bone down to where you want the blouse to end (typically mid-back or lower back, depending on style preference).

5

Sleeve Length

Measure from the shoulder bone to your desired sleeve end - elbow for half sleeves, or wrist for full sleeves.

6

Armhole

Measure around your shoulder and underarm, ensuring the tape goes over the shoulder bone and under the armpit comfortably.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do sarees come in different lengths?

Most of our sarees are 6.3 meters in length including the blouse piece, which is the standard length suitable for most women (5'2" to 5'10"). If you need a longer saree (6.5+ meters), please contact us for custom orders.

What if I'm between sizes for ready-made blouses?

We recommend sizing up if you're between sizes. It's easier to take in a slightly larger blouse than to let out a tight one. Alternatively, opt for the attached unstitched blouse piece for a perfect custom fit.

How much fabric do I need for a custom blouse?

The 0.8-1 meter blouse piece attached to our sarees is sufficient for most blouse designs up to size XXL. For heavily embellished blouses or sizes above XXL, you may need additional fabric.

Can I return a saree if the fit isn't right?

Yes, we offer easy 7-day returns for unused sarees in original condition. However, since sarees are draped garments with standard lengths, fit issues are rare. Please check our return policy for details.

Do you offer plus sizes?

Yes, we offer ready-made blouses in sizes up to 4XL (48-50 inch bust). For larger sizes or custom measurements, please contact our customer service for personalized assistance.
